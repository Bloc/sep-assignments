Using Pseudo-Code - intro to algorithms

Calculating a tip
  READ receipt for waiter
  SET TOTAL = total amount owed on receipt
  CALCULATE TIP = TOTAL * 20%

Stopping at a four way Stop Sign
  IF you are the only car at the intersection THEN
    GO
  ESLE
    IF CAR is present to the right THEN
      Allow car to go through
    ELSE
      Look to left and proceed with Caution
    ENDIF
  ENDIF

Is the garbage ready to go out?
  WHILE garbage bag isn't full
    PLEASE garbage in bag
  ENDWHILE
  DISPLAY message: Garbage is full, time to take it out.
